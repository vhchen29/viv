I am the ultimate fan of pdf or print portfolios. I enjoy getting to customize my work for each recipient and working with a static medium. It’s convenient, lightweight, and quick to review.

However, I do admit the pdf portfolio has some drawbacks. Linking a video content feels like an extra step similar to opening a project on a website, and not to mention many applications today only accept a url and not a file upload.

So, I’ve decided to make my own pdf viewer largely based on my favorite, google drive.
